ABOUT

Protocore is a rapid-prototyping studio based in Leeuwarden, Friesland. We turn ideas into real, functional products — fast.

With in-house capabilities including FDM and resin 3D printing, CNC routing, electronics development, and mechanical assembly, we build and test physical prototypes from first sketch to working result.

Whether you're exploring a new concept, improving an existing product, or ready to build and iterate, Protocore provides the tools, engineering, and hands-on experience to make it real.
